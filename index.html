TR Roberts

TR explores the overlaps of art, cocktail culture, design and music in queer nightlife. A relative of a former gay bar owner in 1990s Houston, Texas, TR investigates their personal connections to queer history through self-directed research and archiving. Graduating from the Milwaukee Institute of Art & Design’s Integrated Studio Arts program in 2014, their artwork has been exhibited in Milwaukee galleries Center-Center, Frederick Layton and Inova with features in publications by GenderFail, Library of the Printed Web, and Soft Daddy. TR has freelanced as a graphic designer for clients like Fade to Mind, GenderFail, Luar and The New School. On Chicago’s Issa Party label, TR has released music as Steamroom and DJ’ed for Midwest queer parties Earth Angel and Deep Lavender.

TR is based in North Carolina where they currently volunteer at the LGBTQ Center of Durham. At the center TR runs programming engaging those who wish to find community, chosen family and connections in the area. TR has previously worked in talent, recruiting and education. From 2015 to 2022, they served as an admission counselor at The New School’s Parsons School of Art and Design. For Parsons, TR has lectured on admissions portfolio preparation and developed workshops for writing college admissions essays. They have supported students from around the world studying in competitive secondary school arts programs across the US and Canada like Chicago High School for the Arts, Dreyfoos School of the Arts, Design and Architecture Senior High, Etobicoke School of the Arts, Interlochen Center for the Arts, Kinder High School for the Performing and Visual Arts, Marwen, New World School of the Arts, and Perpich Center for Arts Education.
